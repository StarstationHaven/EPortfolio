About Me

Doctoral Student at University of Florida: College of Education

Biography

Hello, my name is Idrissa Presley-Peterson. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I am a graduate research assistant, graphic designer, and event organizer. I am pursuing a doctorate in Education Technology; my previous degrees were Information Technology with a focus in project management. My research interests are in game-based learning, integrations of technology in STEAM education, and collaborative online environments.


My goal is to implement my research findings into the education system and make lasting change for innovative and inclusive education as an academic administrator.
